We are glad that you are here!

We know how difficult and daunting it can be to seek help. Our aim is to simplify this process without making any kind of compromises.

In our Managed Care approach, we make sure that you are assisted in your journey. We have a dedicated team of mental health professionals that work together to ensure your comfort and convenience.

A psychologist will connect with you in the first call, and based on their recommendations and your decision, we will plug-in a psychiatrist if required. No multiple hops anymore!

Testimonials

Archana

“Most people around me didn’t understand the unseen struggle I was going through. Most of them were unaware of the pain I was hiding behind my smiles and posts on social media. Most of them thought anxiety is normal and depression is a part of life. Most of them were busy facing their own fears. But now, somehow those conversations with my therapist, my opening up about my challenges have done enough good and made my efforts towards well-being successful …because now a young friend of mine came to me and said, “I saw you overcoming your challenges, can you help me too?”.

Satya

"At 27, crying is now only a momentary release. It doesn’t represent any prolonging feeling because there are none. Whenever I cry about anything or burst into tears, the only thing it represents is that I care. I care about sexual assaults, lonely kittens, broken children, wounded women, injustice, and inequity. The only thing my tears tell me is that I care and that I have compassion. And that it is a good, good thing.”
